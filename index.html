Earth's Satellite

The moon, Earth's celestial companion, is a captivating spherical body that orbits our planet. Its crater-covered surface reflects the sun's light, creating mesmerizing phases from crescents to full moons.

Distance from Earth

The average distance from the Moon to Earth is approximately 384,400 kilometers (238,855 miles).

Diameter

The Moon has a diameter of about 3,474 kilometers (2,159 miles), making it about one-fourth the size of Earth.

Gravity

Lunar gravity is about 1/6th of Earth's gravity. If you weigh 100 kilograms on Earth, you would weigh only about 16.6 kilograms on the Moon.

Orbital Period

It takes approximately 27.3 days for the Moon to complete one orbit around Earth.
